🎮 Rock Paper Scissors - AI Hand Tracking Combat Arena
⚔️ About The Game
Rock Paper Scissors - AI Combat Arena is an immersive, real-time hand-tracking game that uses your webcam and AI-powered gesture recognition to detect your hand signs and battle against an AI opponent.

No keyboard or mouse needed—just your hands! The game uses MediaPipe to track your hand landmarks and classify your gesture as Rock ✊, Paper ✋, or Scissors ✌️.

🚀 Features
🎯 Real-Time Hand Tracking - Uses MediaPipe's advanced hand landmark detection

🤖 AI Opponent - Smart random opponent with dynamic responses

⚡ Combat System - 3-second countdown, detection, and result phases

🎨 Cyberpunk UI - Neon-styled interface with real-time feedback

🏆 Score Tracking - Keep track of your wins against the bot

📸 Webcam Integration - Uses your camera for gesture detection

🔄 Gesture Stabilization - History buffer for smooth gesture recognition
